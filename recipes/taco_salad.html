Taco Salad

An easily prepared dish containing meat and vegetables,
taco salad is soon to become one of your favorite easy to make dishes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef/turkey
  • 1 (1 oz.) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1/2 (14.5 oz.) package nacho-flavor tortilla chips (Feel free to substitute chips like Doritos!)
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 (15 oz.) can kidney beans, drained (Feel free to substitute with black beans)
  • 1/2 cup ranch-style salad dressing
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion

Directions

  1. Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat. Cook, stirring to crumble, until well done; drain. Stir in taco seasoning mix. Set aside to cool.
  2. Place chips into a large bowl; crush them into bite-sized pieces. Combine with seasoned meat, cheese, beans, tomatoes, lettuce, and green onions.
  3. Pour dressing on top; toss to coat.
